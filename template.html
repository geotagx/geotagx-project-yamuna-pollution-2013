Green water:Green water is highly eutrophic (carrying a lot of nutrients and being starved of oxygen). The high levels of nutrients cause blooms of algae to grow in the water and are a sign of pollution. The algae starve the river of oxygen causing other species to die off. In some areas of the Yamuna oxygen levels are nearly at 0.
